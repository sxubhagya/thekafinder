Access Required

To locate the nearest liquor store and align the compass needle, we need location and motion access.

How to Enable on iOS Safari:

  1. Open iPhone Settings.
  2. Go to Privacy & Security > Location Services and ensure it is turned ON.
  3. Scroll down, select Safari Websites, and choose While Using the App.
  4. Go back to main Settings, select Safari, scroll down to Motion & Orientation Access and toggle it ON.
